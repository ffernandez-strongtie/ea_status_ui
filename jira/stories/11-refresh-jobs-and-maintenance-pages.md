# Story 11: Refresh Jobs And Maintenance Pages

## Value Statement

As a StatusUI administrator, I want Jobs and Maintenance pages to use the refreshed visual system while preserving their current operational information.

## Details For Developers

Refresh Jobs and Maintenance pages using shared page, toolbar, table, and detail components. Jobs must preserve production fields and filters for job id, job name, job description, customer, received date, start/end, and search. Maintenance must preserve production system setting fields and values.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Jobs list displays production job log fields.
- Jobs filters preserve start, end, job name, and job description behavior.
- Job detail page remains reachable and displays production detail fields.
- Maintenance page displays existing system setting names and values.
- Both pages are responsive and accessible.
